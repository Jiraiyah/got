Game Of Thrones, Winter Is Coming (GOT-WIC), is an online, realtime, strategy game. Like any game in this type, you need to actively play it by hours. Days come and go, and you are still playing it. This game will become addictive and you will never ever get tired of playing it. You will start playing by one server, and after few months or a year, not only maintaining the older one, but will open a new account and play in a new fresh server.

When it comes to ROS game type, knowledge is the power. Every single step you take, will make you more powerful, or, can cause harm by slowing you down in long run. Every step you take in game, you need to think deep, ask yourself why are you taking that step, how can it cause your game play in long run.

The aim for this website (google webpages) is to provide you with as much knowledge as you need to not only play GOT-WIC easily, but at the same time, will help you understand what is going on under the hood and what are the game mechanics and how you can identify indications for these mechanics in the game, how can you utilize them for yourself, and how to go beyond game mechanics and affect your enemies in such a way that they could have never thought about it.

Under the tutorial section, You will find posts just like a normal weblog, listed as we post them. I highly recomend reading them from the first post forward. If in future you would need any particular knowledge, it shouldn't be hard to find them using the search button on top of every page.
